feat: Add complete Television inventory management system

Implements full TV advertising inventory with feature parity to other media types (Radio, Streaming, Podcast).

## Features Added

### TV Station Management
- Add/Edit/Delete TV stations with complete metadata:
  * Call Sign (WLS-TV, WMAQ-TV, etc.)
  * Channel Number
  * Network Affiliation (ABC, NBC, CBS, FOX, Independent)
  * Coverage Area (Chicago DMA, etc.)
  * Average Weekly Viewers
  * Station ID tracking

### TV Ad Opportunity Management
- Create/Edit/Delete/Clone TV advertising spots
- Comprehensive ad configuration:
  * Ad Name and Format (30s spot, 60s spot, sponsored segment, etc.)
  * Daypart selection (Prime Time, Daytime, Early Morning, Late Night, Weekend, Sports)
  * Duration options (15s, 30s, 60s, 90s, 120s)
  * Technical specs (Video Format: MPEG2, H.264, ProRes; Resolution: 1080p, 720p, 4K)
  * Performance metrics (Spots/Month, Viewers/Spot)

### Pricing & Revenue
- Multiple pricing models:
  * Per-spot pricing (/spot)
  * Weekly packages (/week)
  * Monthly packages (/month)
  * Contact for pricing
- Frequency-based volume discounts (1x, 4x, 12x, 52x tiers)
- Hub-specific pricing with custom rates
- Automatic monthly revenue calculation: price × spots/month
- Revenue aggregation in Dashboard Overview

### UI/UX Improvements
- Station cards display:
  * Total monthly revenue badge
  * Channel, Network, Viewers, Total Spots count
- Ad cards show green revenue & metrics box:
  * Monthly Revenue (calculated)
  * Spots/Month
  * Viewers/Spot
- Consistent design with other inventory types
- Edit/Clone/Delete controls on hover

### Documentation
- Added comprehensive Television section to pricing-formulas.html (461 lines)
- Created TV_INVENTORY_ANALYSIS.md reference guide
- Added TV_IMPLEMENTATION_SUMMARY.md with full implementation details
